About

The IEEE Vehicular Technology Society (VTS) Japan Chapter, Singapore Chapter, Taipei Chapter, and Seoul Chapter are cooperatively hosting Asia Pacific Wireless Communications Symposium (APWCS) every year.
This symposium aims at providing the platform for researchers from the Asia Pacific area to share fresh results, call for comments or collaborations and exchange innovative ideas of the leading edge research in wireless technologies.

Welcome Message

Welcome Message from the General Chairs

It is our great pleasure to welcome you to the 14th IEEE Vehicular Technology Society Asia Pacific Wireless Communications Symposium (IEEE VTS APWCS 2017) to be held at Incheon National University campus in Incheon Songdo International City, a City Embracing the Future.
APWCS 2017 is technically sponsored by the IEEE VTS, and jointly sponsored by the IEEE VTS Seoul Chapter, Japan Chapter, Taipei Chapter, and Singapore Chapter. APWCS has pursued promoting information exchange and improving collaboration among researchers, scholars, engineers, and leaders working in the wireless communications field, especially, across Asia and Pacific Regions.
This year, APWCS celebrates its 14th year and the enthusiasm of the Technical Program Committee (TPC) created an excellent technical program with special sessions demonstrating the state of the art, and invited talks to be given by brilliant experts in wired and wireless communications. We are grateful for the efforts of all TPC members. Also, we would like to thank to the Organizing Committee members, the invited speakers, the special session organizers, the reviewers, the APWCS Board of Governors, and, last but not least, the authors for their contribution and support.
We hope you will enjoy the technical program, make friendship and experience the cultural varieties of Korea, here, in Incheon Songdo.

Welcome Message from the Technical Program Committee Chairs

On behalf of the Technical Program Committee (TPC), it is our great pleasure to welcome you to the 2017 IEEE Vehicular Technology Society Asia Pacific Wireless Communications Symposium (IEEE VTS APWCS 2017) taking place at Incheon National University in Incheon Songdo International City, Republic of Korea.
This year, we have organized 2 plenary sessions (one keynote speech and four invited talks), 20 regular oral sessions and 4 special sessions for the symposium.
In the plenary sessions, following the symposium traditions, we have invited Dr. Kyungwhoon Cheun (Executive Vice President, Next Generation Communications Business Team, IT and Mobile Communications Division, Samsung Electronics Co., Ltd, Korea) as a keynote speaker, from Seoul Chapter, and invited four speakers, Prof. Heung-No Lee (Gwangju Institute of Science and Technology, Korea), Dr. Sun Sumei (Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R), Singapore), Prof. Li-Chun Wang (National Chiao Tung University, Taiwan), and Mr. Akira Matsunaga (Senior Director, Mobile Network Technical Development Division Technology Sector, KDDI Corporation, Japan) for the invited talks. They will give us exciting and inspiring presentations on wide views of current and future wireless communication systems.
We have selected 95 outstanding papers, including 18 invited papers for the special sessions. After extensive and strict peer-reviews, we have accepted 77 high-quality papers that cover a broad range of important and timely issues related to state-of-art wireless communications technologies. These papers have been grouped into 20 sessions. We have also organized 4 special sessions comprising of 18 invited papers aiming at sharing the ideas of new technical trends among all the participants in the symposium.
The technical program would not be possible without voluntary support from an outstanding team of colleagues that we would like to strongly thank.
Of course, making a successful symposium is not possible without active participation of the paper authors. We would like to express our gratitude to them for having decided to share their valuable ideas and contributions with other participants from the community. Finally, we would like to express our gratitude to the IEEE VTS APWCS 2017 Organizing Committee members for their full support.

Information for Authors

Guidelines for Presentations

Each presenter has 20 minutes: 17 minutes for presentation and 3 minutes for Q&A. Session chairs will be very strict about timing and will stop you when your time is up even if you have not finished. Strict adherence to timing within sessions is important in order to allow attendees to move between sessions.

Furthermore, every presenter is required to show up 10 minutes early before the session begins. Upon your arrival to the session, please submit your brief biography to the session chair and upload your presentation material on the laptop in the session room. It is recommended to prepare the presentation material in both powerpoint and pdf formats.
